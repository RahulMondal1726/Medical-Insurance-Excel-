# Medical-Insurance-Excel-
The dataset contains detailed information about medical insurance policyholders, including demographic, socio-economic, lifestyle, and health-related variables. It helps analyze the relationship between individual characteristics (age, gender, employment status, BMI, etc.) and their insurance choices, costs, and claims. Data appears to be collected from different regions (Central, East, North, South, West) and covers multiple plan types (HMO, PPO, POS, EPO). 

Key Fields in Dataset: 
Demographic Variables: age, sex, region, urban-rural classification, household size, dependents.

Socio-economic Variables: income, education, employment, marital status.

Health Variables: bmi, smoking, alcohol, visits_last_year, hospitalizations_last_year, medications.

Insurance Variables: plan type, network_tier, deductible, co_pay, policy_term, policy_changes, risk_score, annual medical cost, annual premium, claims count, total_claims_amount.

Dataset Size: Approximately 95,618 records with a wide range of insurance parameters.
